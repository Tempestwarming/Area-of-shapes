Calculate the Estimated Mean

Class Interval Frequency (f) Midpoint (x) f × x
Final Estimated Mean =

(Total f×x ÷ Total Frequency)

The Method

1. Midpoint: Find the middle of the interval (Add them and divide by 2).
2. Multiply: Multiply Frequency by Midpoint for each row.
3. Totals: Add up the Frequency column AND the fx column.
4. Divide: Final Total fx ÷ Total Frequency.
